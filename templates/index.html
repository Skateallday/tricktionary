What we do

This is your one stop shop for finding out about Professional Skaters, Skateboard Companies, Wheels, Shoes and Trucks and even learn a trick or two.

Skating is an ever changing sport, with new pros coming out every year and people dropping off or changing teams. This site is a platform to track where they are currently.

This site is still under development. If you have any suggestions regarding the design or site features feel free to drop us a message in the contact form and we can take it from there.

We are open to new cases, if anything has been missed hit up the contact form and we'll add it to the site.
